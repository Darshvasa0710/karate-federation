Training Overview

At Supreme Arts Shito-Ryu Karate-Do Federation Of India, our training programs are carefully designed to develop strength, discipline, confidence, and technical excellence.

Whether you are a beginner or an advanced practitioner, our structured curriculum ensures steady progress at every level. We combine traditional Shito-Ryu techniques with modern training methods for self-defense and competitive success.

Training Objectives

💪 Build strength, flexibility & endurance
🧠 Develop mental focus & discipline
🛡 Learn effective self-defense
⚡ Improve coordination & reflexes
🏆 Prepare for competitions

Training Levels

🟢 Beginner Level

Perfect for new students starting their journey.

  • Basic stances (Kihon)
  • Fundamental punches & kicks
  • Basic blocks
  • Discipline & etiquette

🟡 Intermediate Level

For students who have mastered basics.

  • Advanced combinations
  • Speed & timing improvement
  • Kata introduction
  • Basic sparring (Kumite)

🔴 Advanced Level

For experienced practitioners.

  • Advanced kata & bunkai
  • High-level sparring
  • Strength & conditioning
  • Competition preparation
