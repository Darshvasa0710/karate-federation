About Our Federation

Supreme Arts Shito-Ryu Karate-Do Federation Of India

Introduction

Supreme Arts Shito-Ryu Karate-Do Federation Of India is a premier martial arts organization dedicated to the promotion, development, and preservation of traditional Shito-Ryu Karate across India.

Built on the principles of discipline, respect, and excellence, our federation provides a structured and professional environment for individuals of all ages to learn and grow.

We believe that Karate is not just a sport—it is a way of life that shapes character, builds confidence, and strengthens both mind and body.

Our History

Founded with a vision to spread authentic Shito-Ryu Karate, our federation has grown into a respected name in the martial arts community.

Over the years, we have trained numerous students, conducted successful tournaments, and built a strong network of certified instructors and affiliated training centers.

Our journey reflects dedication, hard work, and a passion for excellence in martial arts.

Our Mission

Promote traditional Shito-Ryu Karate in its purest form
Develop discipline, confidence, and leadership
Provide high-quality training at all levels
Encourage competition participation
Build a strong Karate community in India

Our Vision

Our vision is to become a leading Karate federation recognized nationally and internationally for excellence in training, professionalism, and contribution to martial arts.

We aim to produce world-class athletes and responsible individuals who carry forward the values of Karate-Do.

What is Shito-Ryu Karate?

Shito-Ryu Karate is one of the major traditional styles of Karate, known for its combination of powerful techniques and fluid movements. It blends both hard and soft techniques, making it versatile and effective.

Strong emphasis on kata (forms)
Balanced offensive & defensive techniques
Speed, precision, and control
Deep-rooted traditional values
